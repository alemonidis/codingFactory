Why join our platform

Concert organizers never take the account the thoughts and hopes of thousands of music lovers who are anxiously waiting for their favorite band to visit their country.Instead, they only think about maximizing profit and cater to the music tastes of very specific groups. If you're as tired of this as we are, simply join us here and take matters in your hands!

How it works

In order for us to be able to negotiate with the artists, we need to reach a certain budget target (different for each artist).
For every one of you who petitions for a band, we need you to donate a certain amount so that we can reach the specified budget. In case that your desired artist comes for a concert, your donation will be discounted from the concert ticket via a voucher! In case that we dont't succeed within of 6 months of your donation, you will be reimbursed with the full amount!
